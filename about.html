Our Story

From One Technician to Harare's Trusted Print Partner

Topiary Marketing (Private) Limited was incorporated on 22 February 1999 under the Companies Act (Chapter 24:03). From the start, the company focused on supplying and servicing computer consumables, printers, photocopiers, office stationery and hardware — meeting a clear need in Harare's business community for reliable, high-quality products at competitive prices.

The solution was simple in concept: build a business that could respond fast, fix things properly, and supply quality consumables without businesses having to wait days or import at great cost.

Over the years, the team grew and the product range expanded to include toner cartridges, ink cartridges, laptops, photocopiers, office stationery, hardware and electrical items. Today, Topiary Marketing proudly serves businesses across Harare — from small law firms to large corporate offices, schools, and government departments.
