About Us

Zimbabwe's Most
Trusted Office Solutions Partner

Since 1999, Topiary Marketing (Pvt) Ltd has been Harare's go-to cartridge supplier for businesses across Zimbabwe seeking premium toner cartridges, ink cartridges, printing consumables, hardware, and office essentials. Based at Electra House in Harare, we serve clients from all sectors with dedication and expertise.

We source genuine and high-yield compatible products from the world's leading brands — ensuring your business never misses a beat. Our commitment to quality, value, and exceptional service has earned us a reputation built on integrity.
